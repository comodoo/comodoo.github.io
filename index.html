The Comodoo Project is for us the next generation of open source software platform for the commerce through the Point of Sale as the primary driver for Innovation. The Comodoo Project puts together the commerce devices, the Data Center backend for massive scaling and the insights guided by analytics to maximize the store sales.

POS Applications

A comprehensive suite of business applications (ERP) with a powerful module architecture. Being Odoo Point of Sales module our project foundation.

Embedded Technology

Yocto Project is the base technology for our specializated Operating System for in-shop devices, included the Point of Sale, energy control, sound, video and infotainments.

IOT Technology

Approaching the in-shop devices as an Internet Of things use of case, brings our side a full innovate field. Our device operating system based on Yocto is specializated towards IoT using ResinOS (a yocto layer for IoT).

PaaS at Scale

The Point of Sale and derived are limited compute powered, the powerful brain of these devices is focused in a Data Center backend able to support thousand of shops. Apache Mesos flavor from Mesosphere is our best election.
